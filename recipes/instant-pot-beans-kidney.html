Instant Pot Kidney Beans

Dried kidney beans cooked to perfection in less time than it took to complete the first Odin recipes project.

Ingredients

  • Kidney beans
  • Water or chicken stock

  1. Place dried beans in steamer basket.
  2. Cover beans with water, chicken broth, or combination of both.
  3. Pressure cook on HIGH for 25 minutes.
  4. Natural release for 15 minutes, then manual release.
