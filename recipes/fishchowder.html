Fish Chowder

Easy from-scratch fish chowder. Recipe by Lyn from Baja.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup each chopped celery, onion and green pepper
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 can (14-1/2 ounces) chicken broth
  • 1 cup uncooked diced peeled potatoes
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 to 3/4 teaspoon dill weed
  • Â½ cup corn cut off thee cob
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 1-3/4 to 2 cups marlin chunks or other fish

Instructions

  1. Add all the ingredients except the cream and fish together and cook until done.
  2. Add the cream and marlin and cook another 15 minutes.
